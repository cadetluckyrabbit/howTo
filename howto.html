How to Crochet a Womens Hat

Band Crown Hat Sides

Materials

  • 1 skein Lion Brand Scarfie(colorway pictured:Pink/Silver)
  • 6.5 mm Crochet Hook
  • Yarn Needle
  • Scissors

Abbreviations

  • Bpdc back post double crochet
  • Ch chain
  • Dc double crochet
  • Fpdc front post double crochet
  • Rem remaining
  • Slst slip stitch
  • St(s)stitch(es)

Crown

  1. Ch 4, 7 Dc in 4th ch from hook. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.
  2. Ch 3. Make 2 Dc in each st to last. Dc in same st as ch 3. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(16)sts
  3. Ch 3. Make 2 dc in first st. *1 dc in next st. 2 dc in next st. Rep from * to end of round. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(24)sts
  4. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in first st. *2 dc in next st. 1 dc in next 2 sts. Rep from * to last. 2 dc in last st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(32)sts
  5. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in first 2 sts. *2 dc in next st. 1 dc in next 3 sts. Rep from * to last. 2 dc in last st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(40)sts
  6. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in first 3 sts. *2 dc in next st. 1 dc in next 4 sts. Rep from * to last. 2 dc in last st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(48)sts
  7. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in first 4 sts. *2 dc in next st. 1 dc in next 5 sts. Rep from * to last. 2 dc last st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(56)sts
  8. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in first 5 sts. *2 dc in next st. 1 dc in next 6 sts. Rep from * to last. 2 dc last st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(64)sts

Hat sides

  1. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in each st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch(64)sts
  2. Rep last round 2 more times

Band

  1. Ch 3. Make 1 dc in each st. Join with a slst to 3rd ch.(64)sts
  2. Ch 2 (does not count as st). *Fpdc, Bpdc. Rep from * to end of round. Join with a slst to first Fpc.
  3. Rep last round 4 more times.
    Fasten off an weave in your ends.
